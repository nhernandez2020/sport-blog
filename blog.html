Leo Santa Cruz is schedulled to fight Gervonta ‘Tank’ Davis on October 24. Leo Santa Cruz has made it very clear that it's going to cost Davis if he fails to make weight. We have seen Gernonta "tank Davis miss weight in the past, but according to Davis it's because he has started training camps late and has only been training for 8 weeks. Davis is scheduled to train for 12 weeks this time around to get ready for Leo Santa Cruz.

Santa Cruz and Tank Davis are fighting at the Mohegan Sun Casino in Uncasville, Connecticut.The main event will be telivied by Showtime PPV. The winner will hold WBA titles at both 130 and 135. If Davis win he is likely going to vaccate the 130 pound tiltle and continue to fight at 135 as that is his more natural weight.
